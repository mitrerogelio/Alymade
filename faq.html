Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I place an order?
A: To place an order inquiry please go to the “order” section and fill out the request. I will respond as quickly as I can with a quote.

Q: How much notice do I need to order cookies?
A: At least 2-3 weeks notice is appreciated.

Q: What kind of sugar cookies do you make?
A: Vanilla, Chocolate, and Chocolate Chip Sugar Cookies. (Chocolate and Chocolate Chip have an additional cost due to the extra ingredients).

Q: Do you make allergen-free cookies?
A: I currently do not offer any special diet cookies (gluten-free, sugar-free, dairy-free). My recipe does not contain nuts, however the cookies are not made in a peanut-free kitchen.

Q: What do your prices start at?
A: Basic custom cookies start at $4/cookie or $42 a dozen. Exact pricing depends on the number of colors, detail, words or characters that you would like on your cookies. A 50% deposit is due once it is confirmed that I am able to complete your request. Your balance is due the day of pick up. If you are doing contactless porch pick-up, the order will not be placed outside until the payment is received.

Q: What happens if I need to cancel my order, do you offer refunds?
A: Your 50% deposit is refundable if canceled more than 1 week before your pick up date.

Q: Do you ship your cookies? Do you deliver?
A: Under the Minnesota Cottage Law, I am unable to ship my cookies. At this time, I do not deliver. Local pick-up only in Shakopee, Minnesota.

Q: How long do the cookies last? And how do I store them?
A: I heat seal all of my cookies and that helps them stay fresh for up to 3 weeks. If they are out of the bags, store the cookies in an airtight container and place in a cool dry area away from direct sunlight.

Q: Can you freeze decorated cookies?
A: Yes! To freeze, just carefully place your wrapped cookies in a container and freeze. Once you are ready to eat them, just set them out on your counter the night before to thaw out. Do not open the container until they are fully thawed.
